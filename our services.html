🛩️ Remote Pilot Licence (RePL) Drone Training Course

Become a Certified Drone Pilot – Learn, Fly, and Get Licensed!

Course Overview

Our REPL Drone Training Course is designed for individuals and professionals who want to legally operate drones (over 2kg) for commercial purposes. This comprehensive course will equip you with the knowledge and hands-on experience needed to become a CASA-certified drone pilot.

🎯 Who Is This Course For?

  • Aspiring commercial drone operators
  • Surveyors, photographers, and filmmakers
  • Real estate, agriculture, and mining professionals
  • Students entering the drone industry
  • Anyone wanting to operate drones professionally

📚 What You Will Learn

  • Aviation knowledge and air law
  • Meteorology and risk assessment
  • Navigation and flight planning
  • Operating procedures and battery management
  • Emergency procedures and safety protocols
  • Practical flight training with multirotor drones

Course Details

Total Duration: 12 weeks

Mode: In-person / Hybrid

Location: Gokarna

Prerequisite: Age 16+, +2 Mandatory

🎓 Certification

Upon successful completion, students will receive:

  • Remote Pilot Licence (RePL) from Drone Guru Pvt. Ltd
  • Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate (AROC) – If applicable
  • Official logbook and training records

  • Drone Operator for Agriculture & Surveying
  • Drone Photographer/Videographer
  • Drone Inspector for Infrastructure Projects
  • Emergency Response & Environmental Monitoring
  • Freelance UAV Contractor
